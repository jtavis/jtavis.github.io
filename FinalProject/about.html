This site was developed as a final project for a front end web development course at General Assembly.

I developed it because many government, nonprofit, and religious organizations spend extensive time and resources assembling directories of neighborhood services.

By marrying the IRS nonprofit database (which has every organization that has nonprofit status in the US) with Google Maps, I hoped to make the job of coming up with those directories a little bit easier.

To accomplish this, I made use of the nonprofit API published by ProPublica (because the IRS only offers CSV files) and the Google Maps API.
